SmartNet Uncapped blockchain routers

We are bringing the sharing economy and blockchain's encryption to wifi networks. We give wifi owners the opportunity to sell off some of their data(bandwidth) for people who are within the routers radius.
This allows people to enjoy their usual data at prices that get lower as more people join the network. This allows people to turn one of the biggest financial burdens(data) into an opportunity to make an income
